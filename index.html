Mission

Fur Friends is a not-for-profit 503(c) organization
dedicated to the health and welfare of animals
that owns and operates a shelter
for unwanted and stray dogs, and cats,
as well as the occasional hamster,
guinea pig and rabbit, in Essex, Vermont.
